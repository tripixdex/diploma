Conveyor Maintenance


1. Belt tensions should be checked for undue slackness and adjusted with the 2 x M6 nuts on the RHS of each of the 6 motor housings

2. The air supply drain should be emptied periodically

3. Conveyor corner "Ball Transfer Units" may need adjusting if pallets keep getting stuck at the conveyor corners

4. Pallet station sensors may need adjusting if pallets are not detected (See Interface Module LEDs)
