ASRS Maintenance


1. All 6  Linear slide rails must be kept clean and free from dirt and lubricated frequently with light Engineering oil

2. Belt tensions should be checked periodically to ensure they are not slack

3. The Felt wipers on each of the 4 carriages (4 Wipers per carriage) will need cleaning and lubricating with light Engineering oil

4. The Fan on the control box should be checked periodically to ensure it is running

5. The Filter on the control box should be checked periodically to ensure it is not clogged up

6. Home and over travel sensors may need adjusting if the ASRS will not HOME
