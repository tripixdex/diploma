# First Lab Visit Checklist

This is the quick field companion for the first lab visit. The source of truth remains:
- `hardware_survey_packet.md`
- `first_lab_data_capture_sheet.md`
- `board_fact_checklist.md`
- `lab_no_go_actions.md`
- `first_lab_expected_artifacts.md`

## Before Leaving
- Print or open the survey packet.
- Prepare the data capture sheet.
- Prepare a photo-capable device.
- Confirm the visit is a survey mission only.
- Confirm that no control, GPIO, or motion experimentation is planned.

## On Arrival
- Record date, location, participants, and supervisor.
- Ask what is allowed and what is forbidden before touching anything.
- Confirm whether the observed board is really Raspberry Pi 4, something else, or still uncertain.
- Confirm whether Orange Pi is relevant at all in the actual lab setup.

## Before Touching Hardware
- Photograph overall board context.
- Photograph board markings and connectors.
- Photograph power-entry and ESTOP-related areas if visible.
- Record unknowns explicitly instead of guessing.
- Stop immediately if power or ESTOP boundaries are unclear.

## Before Leaving The Lab
- Ensure board facts are captured.
- Ensure AGV-side power / connector / interface observations are captured.
- Ensure allowed vs forbidden actions are written down.
- Ensure unknowns are explicitly listed.
- Ensure the expected artifact set is complete or incompleteness is recorded honestly.
